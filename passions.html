Professional Commitment to Insurance and Finance

Hello, I'm Ny-Jianah Zeigler, and I'm passionate about forging a successful career as an insurance agent and financial advisor. My journey in these fields has been marked by dedication, resilience, and a constant pursuit of excellence.

I commenced my career as a life insurance agent with American Income Life of Globe Life. While the beginning was interrupted by the challenges of commissions-based income during the early stages of the pandemic, I honed essential skills that form the foundation of my professional identity.

During my seven months as an insurance agent, I developed expertise in drive, motivation, negotiation, communication, sales, presentation, public speaking, client relationship management, contract binding, rapport building, scheduling, and various aspects of client policy management.

My commitment to continuous improvement led me to a role as a tutor, where I organized and networked to earn $600 a week for 24 hours of schooling. Subsequently, I transitioned into the fast-paced environment of the food industry, climbing the ranks from a crew member to assistant manager within six months.

As the world emerged from the pandemic, I sought a role as a water consultant at AquaLiteUS. There, I not only contributed to a substantial increase in company revenue but also managed a team of home depot canvassers, demonstrating my leadership and strategic thinking skills.

Driven by the desire to further excel, I am pursuing licensing and education in finance and insurance, aiming for certifications such as SIE, Series 7, and Series 63. These endeavors reflect my commitment to continuous learning and skill development.

I am now seeking a company that recognizes and nurtures potential, offering a platform for me to contribute significantly to the success of the business while achieving my professional goals. Let's embark on this journey together!
